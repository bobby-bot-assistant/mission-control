# This Week in Social Media Litigation
## Week 6, 2026 (February 3-9)
### Trial Week 2: Meta & YouTube Defense Begins

---

## Executive Summary

The second week of the landmark social media mental health trial in LA County Superior Court saw the defense begin presenting their case. Key developments include internal documents revealing Meta knew about addiction risks, testimony from former YouTube engineers about recommendation algorithms, and growing speculation about settlement negotiations following TikTok and Snapchat's pre-trial settlements.

**Why This Matters for Mindful Media:** Every revelation strengthens our positioning as "the antidote to the poison." Courts are validating the exact harms we've been building solutions for.

## Key Developments This Week

### Monday, February 3
- **Defense Opening:** Meta's lawyers argue features were designed for "engagement, not addiction"
- **Key Quote:** "We gave users what they wanted - connection and community"
- **MM Angle:** They're admitting to optimizing for engagement over wellbeing

### Thursday, February 6

### Friday, February 7 (Projected)
