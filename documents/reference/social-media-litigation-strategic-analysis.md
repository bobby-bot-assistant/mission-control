## ðŸ’° POTENTIAL SETTLEMENTS & CY PRES AWARDS

### What Are Cy Pres Awards?

**Definition:** Distribution of money from class action settlements to charitable organizations when individual distribution to class members is impractical or impossible.

**How They Work:**
- Class action settles for $X million/billion
- If individual payouts are too small to distribute ($10/person), or if class members can't be identified/located
- Court directs funds to nonprofits that serve the interests of the harmed class

**Perfect for This Case Because:**
1. Class is HUGE (millions of affected children)
2. Individual harm varies widely (hard to calculate per-person damages)
3. Future harm is ongoing (children still using platforms)
4. Preventative solutions needed (not just retroactive payments)
